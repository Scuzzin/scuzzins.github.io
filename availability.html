PARTY PRICING

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking, if your party is cancelled, you can reschedule. Should the deposit not be paid within a week of booking, we will be open to taking another booking for that slot. Confirmation of number of children attending is required a week before the party and full payment is then due via EFT. Should your party exceed the 3 hour time slot you will be charged a R300 fee per half hour extra time.
